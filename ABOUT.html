Founded in 1992 in Dehiwala, Sri Lanka, NOLIMIT began as a single store with just five employees.
Over the years, we've embraced change and growth, evolving into a global fashion powerhouse.
Our journey from a single flagship store to a network of over 28 stores across Sri Lanka is a testament to our dedication to providing accessible, high-quality fashion.
With over 1,500 staff members and a retail footprint spanning 425,800 square feet, NOLIMIT has become Sri Lanka's largest fashion chain.
Beyond our domestic success, NOLIMIT has expanded its reach into the vibrant fashion market of the UAE, solidifying our position as a leading international retailer.
